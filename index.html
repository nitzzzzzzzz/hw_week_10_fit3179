Internet Access Insights

Internet Access by Employment Status

This visualization shows the percentage of people who have internet access based on their employment status. You can observe how being employed full-time or part-time correlates with access to the internet, with insights into how unemployment or being out of the workforce affects internet accessibility.

Internet Access by Household Composition

This chart explores the relationship between household composition (whether a household has children or not) and internet access. It highlights how having children in a household impacts internet access availability compared to those without children in the household.
